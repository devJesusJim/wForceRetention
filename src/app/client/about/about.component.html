Our Story

For over 10 years we’ve been offering powerful insight to organisations large and small. In the early years of testing and learning, I found having the ability to be flexible and offer a very personalised service with objective reporting was so important. The platform now caters for multiple modes of exit interview with features that cater for all kinds of requirements. Whether a combination of online and phone exit interviews, using the platform internally or just to have us expertly interview leavers and provide you with an insightful report.

We specifically focus on the ‘so what’ factor so results are meaningful. This value permeates the entire design of the exit interview process from the questions we ask, through to producing reports.. We specialise in making the experience as professional and engaging as it can be, leaving employees with a sense of fulfilment while providing you with valuable insight for your business. These programs have achieved 95% participation, translating into rich insightful reporting for business leaders.
